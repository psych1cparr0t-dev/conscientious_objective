Preamble

We hold, as our founding conviction, that conscience is not merely a private faculty but a public act — that to examine one's beliefs, to refine them through literature and history and practice, and to stand by them under pressure, is among the most courageous things a person may do.

Conscientious Objective is a peace education initiative. For $2.50 per month, subscribers receive a structured and documented journey through the great traditions of nonviolence, pacifism, and moral refusal — from Thoreau to Gandhi to King, from Quaker theology to contemporary just war critique.

Each month builds upon the last. Each subscriber builds a record — sincere, timestamped, and entirely their own. We make no promise of legal outcome. We offer something older and more durable: a community of conscience. Your documentation, compiled and yours to keep, supports any formal conscientious objection application you choose to pursue.
